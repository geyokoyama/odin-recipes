Stir Fry

Description

This beef and broccoli dish comes together in just 25 minutes and only requires ingredients that you probably already have.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 piece of chicken thigh, cut into bite-size strips
  • 1 cup broccoli
  • 1 cup bell peppers cut into match sticks
  • 1 cup carrots thin sliced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 2 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesami seeds

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat; add chicken and stir-fry until browned, 3 to 4 minutes.
  2. Move chicken to the side of the wok and add broccoli, bell pepper, carrots, green onion, and garlic to the center of the wok; stir-fry vegetables for 2 minutes.kj
  3. Stir chicken into vegetables and season with soy sauce and sesame seeds. Continue to cook and stir until vegetables are tender, about 2 more minutes.
  4. Put into bowl and serve with rice.
