Ramen

Description

Ramen is a Japanese noodle dish. It consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a broth.

Common flavors include soy sauce, miso, salt, topped with sliced meat and fresh vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Instant Noodle
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Green onions
  • pork

Steps

  1. Make instant noodles as instruction
  2. While cooking the noodle, put soy sauce and sesame oil in a bowl
  3. Pour the instant noodle into the bowl
  4. Place green onions and pork on top, and it's ready to eat!
