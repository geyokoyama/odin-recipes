Fried Rice

Description

A simple fried rice with bacon.

Ingredients

  • Sesame Oil
  • White Rice
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Green onions
  • Bacon
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Once rice is cooked, Heat up pan with oil
  3. Dice the bacon and cook it
  4. Batter the eggs
  5. Pour eggs into the pan, once it starts to solidify Put the rice in
  6. Mix the eggs with the rice and put salt & pepper in
  7. Put into a bowl
