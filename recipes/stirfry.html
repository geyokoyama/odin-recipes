Stirfry

Description

Pan fried vegetables with your choice of meat.

Season with salt or any other sauce you enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Sesame Oil
  • Bell Peppers
  • Cabbage
  • Red Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Meat of your choice
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat up pan
  2. Cook meat until thoroughly cooked
  3. Add the rest of the vegetables. Cook until it has the texture of your choice
  4. Season with salt and pepper
  5. Place on plate
